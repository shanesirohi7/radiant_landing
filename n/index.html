Key Features

Radiant offers a range of features tailored to the needs of students:

  • Class and Section-based Groups: Text anyone in pre made class and section based groups.
  • Interest-based Communities: Join groups based on your hobbies, passions, and extracurricular activities.
  • Secure and Private Environment: Interact in a safe and moderated space, protected from unwanted access.
  • Modern and Intuitive Interface: Enjoy a smooth UI with 200+ themes to keep you entertained.
  • Announcements and Events: Stay informed about news related to school clubs/groups and participate in events held in school.
  • Random Match: Get randomly matched with anyone in your school for a fun anonymous conversation, after which you can chose to send a request or not.
  • Anonymous Threads: Share your thoughts on your school in anonymous threads just like reddit.
  • Interactive Anonymous Polls: Create polls on any topic and vote on them anonymously.
  • Leaderboard: Be on top of leaderboards by staying active and engaging with your school.
  • Clubs: Join and stay updated about all the clubs in your school.
  • Badges: Earn badges by participating in events, joining clubs, and staying active on Radiant.
